Continuous Loop Logic

The system is programmed for infinite cycling. Every 28 seconds, the terminal re-authorizes and pushes a fresh command burst (!work, !collect, !dep all) to the Discord API.

Buffer Frequency

Commands are separated by a 100ms buffer. This mimics a rapid-fire macro while ensuring the Discord gateway registers each request as a distinct event.

Operational Warning

Keep this tab active. Browsers may sleep background tabs, which can break the 28-second timer. For 24/7 farming, ensure your PC sleep settings are disabled.
